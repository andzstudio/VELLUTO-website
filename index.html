The Foundation
Born in the heart of Galati, Velluto was never conceived as a mere dining establishment. It was built as a sanctuary of taste. Since our inception, we have laid the cornerstone of what would become the city's undisputed culinary pinnacle.

We pioneered the Nice Dining concept—a deliberate departure from the rigid formalities of traditional fine dining. Our menu is a tapestry where classic techniques meet multi-cultural fusion. Global inspiration executed with local refinement.
